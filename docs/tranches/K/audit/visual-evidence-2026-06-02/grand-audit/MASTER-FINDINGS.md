## §E — Top cross-constellation themes

1. **The configurator-side pattern is contested in 3-4 repos and resolves to ONE glass-ui prop.** fourier (RIGHT→LEFT mandate), muster (F's 4 approaches + the 280px placeholder), speedtest (re-founding), value.js (hand-rolled dual-pane) all hit a Configurator hardcoded stage-left/aside-right (Configurator.vue:103/:131 + DOM order). The KEYSTONE P0: add `asideSide` (+`asideWidth` token), flipping via grid-column placement NOT DOM reorder (preserve tab order). All adopt one prop, delete their bespoke `:deep` grid hacks.

2. **deriveAurora is the single longest chronic and finally clears its ≥2-consumer gate.** glass-ui 3 + value.js 4 tranches. The color math (cssToOklch/oklchToLinear/flattenPalette) + `AuroraConfig.palette` already ship; the ONLY gap is the producer that spins one active color into an N-stop ramp. value.js C2 (K.W4) is the 2nd live consumer (after speedtest's hand-rolled equivalent) → it ships at AS.W5. value.js's atmosphere being palette-blind by construction is the most prominent on-screen motion animating independently of user intent — a purpose-of-animation violation at the wiring layer, not the primitive layer.

3. **Fraunces' SOFT/WONK axes are never engaged anywhere.** glass-ui references Fraunces as `--font-stack-display` and applies `font-variation-settings:"WONK" 1,"SOFT" 0` but ships NO @font-face, so value.js + words + slides each load an axis-incomplete Google-Fonts CDN subset where those settings are silently dropped. The signature soft-wonky character the design system specifies renders generically on every display headline across three apps. Fix at the SOURCE: glass-ui self-hosts a full-axes Fraunces woff2 (the pattern it already uses for Plus Jakarta + Fira Code).

4. **Display↔body type tension is collapsed constellation-wide, and the palettes are timid-grey.** value.js + words alias `--font-serif`=`--font-display`=Fraunces (ornamental serif as 16px body); muster + bbnf collapse all roles to General Sans — the φ-ladder differentiates by size only. Traceable to glass-ui's own default `--accent`/`--primary` being achromatic `--neutral-3`/near-black grey (words is the acute case: cold S=0% ink on warm hue-48 paper). The jewel `--section-color-*` accents exist but aren't the default. Re-found ink on the warm family; commit ONE saturated accent per app.

5. **Reduced-motion coverage is holey on the heaviest motion surfaces.** bbnf-buddy P0 (ZERO PRM across 4 perpetual mascot loops — the worst gap, no opt-out from a continuously-tilting noised mascot), keyframes.js (only light engines gated; the heavy Animation/Group/morph/timeline/waapi drivers that glass-ui /motion wraps have none), fourier (epicycle marquee ungated). One shared engine-base PRM gate in keyframes.js + a reduced-motion-aware RAF primitive that pauses off-screen/tab-hidden is the cross-cutting lever; bbnf needs the 3.1.1 bump to consume it.

6. **CLS-as-un-choreographed-motion clusters on cascade-layering / intrinsic-size reserve.** speedtest P0 (0.3228 — unlayered `min-height:0` defeats the @layer reserve), muster (0.0729 — aside under-reserved + content-visibility placement), slides (the 0.29 letterbox is the extreme: scale-to-fit instead of reflow). All resolve to glass-ui instrument-chassis/Configurator `contain-intrinsic-size` reserve tokens + the discipline "never author unlayered rules touching the sizing of a layered, reserved instrument."

7. **The shared orange-sun + pencil-boil mascot skin wants a glass-ui home.** The skin recurs across fourier, sudoku, and bbnf-buddy (whose MascotMonogram + useMascot is bespoke), with no shared primitive — glass-ui has glyph-face/disco-glyph/animated-digit but no mascot/monogram-pose. Net-new primitive over the glyph-face family (serial after the bbnf 2.0→3.1.1 bump).

8. **friday.institute is VPN-gated and the deploy spine has outliers.** speedtest/slides/words are NXDOMAIN/VPN-gated from the audit network (ground from code + dossiers; book a VPN-side capture at execution); value.js is an rsync outlier vs the git-pull standard; fourier DNS-tuple drift is unverified. The deploy tranche (W6) owns the canonical CSP/security-header + dev.sh/cf-host standard that fourier (`font-src` KaTeX), words (no `_headers`), and value.js all derive from.
