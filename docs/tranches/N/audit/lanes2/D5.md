## §2 — U32: the dock made BIGGER — the density rung is sitting on the shelf

The user's mock (u11, the zoomed "Home ˅ | 🖌 Tools ˅ | ⋮" pill) reads at roughly 1.5× today's
proportions with looser inter-group air. glass-ui's density cascade was BUILT for this — four
rungs, every geometry axis `calc(<base> × --dock-scale)` — and the demo never chose one.

**WO-D5-1 [demo, N.W6.B] — `density="spacious"` + the glyph ride.**
- `Dock.vue:93`: `<GlassDock density="spacious" …>` → padding 0.5/0.75rem, controls+layer 44px,
  gap 0.5rem, trigger pad 0.375/0.625rem, tab-h 44px. Expanded box lands ≈ 320×64; collapsed pill
  ≈ 62px circle. (`audacious` — 64px controls, 4rem layers — is the dock-as-primary-surface rung;
  too much chrome for a shell whose heroes are the cards. `spacious` is the proportioned answer.)
- Glyph proportion: `--dock-icon-glyph` rides ONLY `--dock-scale`, not the density rung
  (`offsets-sizing.css:276`) — at spacious the 44px controls keep 20px glyphs (0.45 ratio; the
  comfortable ratio is 0.50). Demo override on the dock root: `--dock-icon-glyph: 1.375rem` (22px)
  to hold the ratio. **Companion glass-ui ask (NEW, small)**: thread a per-density glyph base
  (`--dock-density-<rung>-icon-glyph`) through the density cascade so consumers don't hand-true it.
- The in-dock Lucide sizes follow free (`w-5 h-5`/`w-6 h-6` slot icons read against the bigger
  plates); the collapsed-slot WatercolorDot `w-6 h-6` (`Dock.vue:198`) → `w-7 h-7`.

**WO-D5-2 [demo, N.W6.B] — hit-target floor.** The slug-edit layer's 22×22 buttons and the 24×24
⋮ trigger sit under the WCAG 2.2 §2.5.8 24px floor (desktop) and far under the 44px coarse floor.
The spacious rung lifts the `DockIconButton`-family controls; the two non-conforming bespoke sizes
(`SlugEditLayer.vue` compact buttons, the ⋮ trigger) move to `DockIconButton compact` minimum 28px
with the coarse-pointer `--dock-control-floor` clamp left to glass-ui (already shipped).

**WO-D5-3 [demo, N.W6.B] — the shell's φ rhythm.** Today: dock at y=8 (a flat 0.5rem), panes
vertically centered with no relationship to it. Prescribe the ladder on the Fibonacci≈φ rungs the
docs lane (D4) uses: `--top-dock-inset: 0.8125rem` (13px rung), pane-container top clearance =
dock-bottom + **34px** (the next-but-one rung), so dock → air → cards step 13/64/34 and the dock
reads as a floating instrument, not a browser-chrome appendage. One token + one container pad —
coordinate with D1/U-cards' two-card desktop grid (U32's layout half is theirs).

**WO-D5-4 [demo, N.W6.B] — first-paint posture.** Desktop boots expanded-at-rest (verified live;
the W6.B viewport-derived seed). KEEP — with `collapse-delay 5000` + collapse-on-idle the dock

so the next audit doesn't re-litigate the `:start-collapsed="false"` choice (`Dock.vue:93`).

## §3 — U6/U16: the dock motion language (on U-DOCK's root cause)

The root cause is OWNED and FILED: the nested-`DockLayerGroup` FLIP measures `to:0px`
deterministically (4/4 cycles), springs the wrong direction 55→19px, dead-holds ~455ms, snaps to
280 in one frame (U-DOCK §1; glass-ui ask **X-GU A-1**, fold into BA `W-DOCK-GEOMETRY`/
`W-DOCK-MORPH-INSITU`, re-scoping the `AY.W-GOD1 §F2` booking). The spring register itself —
`DOCK_SPRING {response:0.32, ζ:0.7}` (`glass-ui constants.ts:32` = the `dock` preset,
`springPresets.ts:74-78`) — is the RIGHT feel and is fenced from re-tune.

What D5 adds — the **motion-language acceptance spec** the W5.D "dock adoptions DONE" claim never
had (it is what let a broken morph ship as green):

**WO-D5-5 [demo e2e, N.W6 gate] — the morph-truth assert.** Fold U-DOCK §5 recipe 1 into
`smoke-reactivity`: drive `collapse()`→settle→`expand()` via the exposed API, sample
`getBoundingClientRect().width` per rAF, assert (a) every frame's width ∈
`[min(from,to)·0.95, max(from,to)·1.05]` — kills wrong-direction travel; (b) no frame-to-frame
jump > 40% of the span — kills the snap; (c) 1%-settle ≤ 450ms — the DOCK register's natural
window. Green only after the glass-ui A-1 fix lands; born-RED is correct and intended.

**WO-D5-6 [demo, N.W6.B] — retire the third substrate.** `Dock.vue:215-227` animates the Tools
toggle reveal by `grid-template-columns: 0fr→1fr` on `--duration-normal --ease-standard` — a CSS
width idiom INSIDE a spring-morphing dock, so the toggle's reveal and the dock's box run on two
unrelated clocks (part of U16's "not sized properly between transitions" read). Retire it onto the
dock's own morph: the toggle is layer content — let the `--dock-morph-t` stagger ramp own its
entrance (the same mechanism the summary glyph rides, `morph.css` W45 DK1), or at minimum re-clock
the grid transition onto `--spring-dock` + its duration companion so the two boxes move as one.

**The choreography contract (post-fix, the design answer in one paragraph).** Expand = ONE
unbroken arc on the dock register: pill 62 → instrument ~320 in ≈350ms with the +4.6% overshoot
kiss; incoming controls reveal ON `--dock-morph-t` (onset-staggered left→right, the ramp glass-ui
already ships) — never after settle, never before the box clears them. Collapse = the inverse;
the summary WatercolorDot lands WITH the pill (W45 DK1 holds). Layer swaps (main↔action-bar↔
slug-edit) ride the same FLIP — one clock for box and content, everywhere, no exceptions. That is
the whole language; it needs zero new mechanism, only the A-1 measurement fix + WO-D5-6.

## §4 — U33 intent: the atmosphere as FRAME

U-AURORA proved the field is alive (webgl, 44fps, advancing uTime) but spatially dead:
`motion:"breathing"` zeroes `nucleiDrift/paletteDrift/warpDrift` (`glass-ui atoms.ts:164-168`),
leaving a ±2.5% luminance pulse. `D5-01` shows the consequence: a flat pink-mauve wash that reads
as a stained background, not an atmosphere. The design intent, post-fix:

**The atmosphere is the FRAME; the cards are glass over it.** A frame must (a) visibly LIVE —
slow spatial drift in the 10–45s contemplative window, hue breathing across the derived analogous
ramp; (b) carry STRUCTURE — 4–5 composed zones whose nuclei give the eye anchors at the field's
edges while the center stays calm under the cards; (c) stay SUBORDINATE — the cards' glass tiers
+ cartoon shadows (U-cards grammar) do the lifting; the field never competes for text contrast.

**WO-D5-7 [demo one-liner, N.W6.B] — `motion: "drifting"` default.** `panes/keys.ts:28`
`"breathing"` → `"drifting"` (nucleiDrift .015 / paletteDrift .015 / warpDrift .008 — the only
register with non-zero spatial life). U-AURORA §4b; `breathing` stays selectable in AuroraPane.

**WO-D5-8 [demo, N.W6.B] — energy floor for the frame read.** `keys.ts:26` `colorEnergy: 0.55 →
0.7` and `zones.count: 4 → 5`. The pale-wash failure (U1's gray contribution) is intrinsic when
the seed is a high-L/low-chroma pick — the derived palette inherits the deadness. The energy lift
is the demo-side half; the register-amplitude half is glass-ui's (**X-GU A-4**, filed: give
`breathing` perceptible life + consider widening the breath half-scale — `aurora.frag.ts:367-368`).

**WO-D5-9 [demo e2e, N.W6 gate] — the temporal gate.** The N.W5 screenshot gate was structurally
blind to motion (U-AURORA §3). Add to smoke: two canvas `readPixels`/screenshot samples 3s apart
on a 16-point grid; assert mean |Δ| ≥ 3/255 on ≥25% of points (drifting is ~an order above this;
breathing-today is ±1–2/255 — the gate separates them). Under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`
the assert inverts (static REQUIRED — glass-ui's `respectReducedMotion` settle).

**WO-D5-10 [verify-at-pin, N.W9] — the dark arm.** glass-ui BA's `W-DARK-MATERIAL`/`W-STAGE`
rebuild the dark register (real ΔL ladder, dark aurora arms). At the 3.13.0+ pin, re-verify the
demo atmosphere in dark mode: the field must deepen (not gray out) and the five glass rungs must
separate over it. No demo work now — a named re-verify so it cannot silently regress.

## §5 — U12: pane choreography + THE NOMENCLATURE STANDARD

### 5a — The vocabulary (the standard U12 demands; → `demo/DESIGN.md` §Motion)

| Term | IS | Transitions via | Register (spring) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **View** | a route (`ViewId`, `viewSchema.ts`) | the view-select / hash | — (it's the cause, not the moved thing) |
| **Pane** | a slot-resident surface (`PaneSlot.vue` content; left/right/mobile slots) | **`pane`** family — directional slide + 2° tilt | enter `--spring-snappy`, leave `--ease-out` |
| **Card** | a glass surface INSIDE a pane (PaletteCard, mix result, swatches) | **`pop`** family — scale 0.96→1 + fade | `--spring-bouncy` (the PLAYFUL one-shot register) |
| **Layer** | an in-dock plane (`DockLayer`) | the dock morph — `--dock-morph-t` | `--spring-dock` |
| **Sheet** | a modal overlay (EditDrawer, dialogs) | **`pop`** (overlay arm) | `--spring-smooth` enter / fast fade leave |
| micro | icon swaps, feedback slivers, field reveals | **`fade`** family — opacity (+≤4px drift) | `--duration-fast --ease-standard` |

Three semantic families. Nothing else gets a `<Transition>` name.

### 5b — The 14→3 disposition (U-DOCK §3 census, dispositioned)

| Today (14 bespoke) | Becomes |
|---|---|
| `pane-left`, `pane-right`, `pane-slide` (App.vue:331-368 — three near-identical blocks) | ONE **`pane`** family; direction via the EXISTING `--pane-slide-dir`/`--pane-slide-rot` custom-prop idiom set per wrapper (App.vue:39,51,66 pass `pane` + the wrapper sets the vars). Delete two CSS blocks. |
| `pop`, `error-pop`, `edit-overlay`, `edit-drawer` | **`pop`** (edit-drawer = the overlay arm) |
| `fade`, `fade-slide`, `eyedropper-fade`, `toggle-icon`, `feedback-slide`, `rename-slide`, `slug-bar-swap`, `swatch-item` | **`fade`** (feedback/rename/slug keep their ≤4px drift inside the family's vars) |

`PaneSlot.vue:35` takes `:name` free-form — after the collapse, type it:
`transitionName: "pane" | "pop" | "fade" | ""` so the vocabulary is CLOSED at the prop boundary
(the same enforcement move L used on the api: the type system, not a grep).

### 5c — The clock fix (the load-bearing motion bug in the shell CSS)

`App.vue:332/344/358` pair `var(--spring-snappy)` with `var(--duration-slow)` (0.45s). A solved
spring `linear()` curve is duration-coupled — riding the snappy curve (response 0.35s, ζ.65) on a
guessed 450ms stretches the overshoot and reads as lag (a quiet contributor to "transitions not
smooth"). The live wire shows `--spring-*-duration` companions EMPTY: glass-ui BA mints them
(`W-TABS`: "the clock on `--spring-snappy-duration`"; `W-GLASS-CAL`'s vocabulary).
**WO-D5-11 [demo, N.W6.B + a verify-at-pin]**: the `pane`/`pop` families consume the paired
`--spring-<name>` + `--spring-<name>-duration` tokens the moment the pin ships them; until then
set the demo's pane clock to the snappy preset's solved settle (≈0.55s for ζ.65/r.35 — take the
exact figure from glass-ui's `regen-spring-tokens.mjs` output, do NOT guess a second time).
NO new glass-ui primitive ask: `useViewTransition`'s async route arm (BA `W-ATLAS-RECONCILE`)
is the eventual route-swap home; the CSS spring pair is the correct KISS bridge.

**WO-D5-13 [demo, N.W6.B] — the menu.** `DockViewSelect.vue:66-91`: rows to ≥40px (`py-2.5
px-3`), label type at trigger parity (U7 — parity holds today at 16.4px; it must SURVIVE the

the 8px gray dot (`:77-79`) is replaced by the WatercolorDot ghost/active pair (U18's variant ask
— active = the live `cssColorOpaque` dot, inactive = the dashed ghost); each row's icon takes its

`pastel-rainbow-text` from the Palettes row (`:88`)** — color lives in the icon+dot system; a

(consumes glass-ui WO-2/D-2, the U23 jerk fix) — the canned `0.15s enter` keyframe dies.

**Acceptance**: trigger ≥20px display-voice type; rows ≥40px; one type-scale step max between
trigger and rows; zero `pastel-rainbow-text` in `demo/`; the open animation reads from the
