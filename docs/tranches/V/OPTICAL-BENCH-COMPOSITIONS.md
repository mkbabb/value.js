# Tranche V — Decided Optical Bench Compositions

**Formation state:** DECIDED · **Implementation:** W17–W30 · **Composition ratification:** W18

## 1. Subject, audience, and single job

value.js is a chromatic laboratory for a person who wants to understand, shape, preserve, and ship a color artifact. Each member has one job: keep the current specimen or decision visually primary while the smallest sufficient instrument explains what may be changed or committed. The spectral meniscus and WatercolorDot are the two characteristic color-bearing forms; neutral glass structure stays quiet enough for them to remain exceptional.

This artifact decides composition before implementation. It covers eleven named first-class Optical Bench routes and seven operational surfaces: the five Admin routes, Account, and unsupported/corrupt storage recovery. W18 turns each row into one named low-fidelity frame and one real-rendered frame; a family frame cannot stand in for a member.

## 2. Non-negotiable frame

The shell owns exactly one `<main>` and one visible H1 for the active route. `InstrumentChassis` is landmark-neutral housing inside that main: its stage/dial region must not emit another `<main>`. Overlays such as Account remain dialogs and never create a competing main landmark.

```text
┌ reserved Dock band ────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
<main>
  route H1 / compact identity
  ┌ primary composition ───────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │ golden stage 61.8033989% │ inspector 38.1966011%               │
  │ or preview 66.6666667%   │ inspector 33.3333333%               │
  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
</main>
```

The split exists only when both regions carry active work. Without an earned inspector, the protagonist expands and the unused region disappears; it is never replaced by a companion Card. At narrow width there is one document-scrolling sequence beneath the Dock, not two simultaneous miniatures. A `Card` houses a bounded entity or specimen, never a page column, empty half, stage wrapper, or padding group.

### Closed member routes

The route inventory is exactly `/` Picker, `/palettes` Library, `/browse` Browse, `/extract` Extract, `/mix` Mix, `/generate` Generate, `/gradient` Gradient, `/easing` Easing, `/atmosphere` Atmosphere, `/blob` Blob, and `/about` About. Each has one first-class Dock affordance and direct-load identity. Easing sits adjacent to Gradient in navigation but owns a separate route/chassis; About occupies the quiet trailing information position and never returns as Picker's companion. W19 owns route/Dock/title/focus behavior, while each row below owns its body.

## 5. Boundary, reserve, spacing, and producer/consumer seams decided here

The Card inventory is terminal: Browse and Library use exactly one Card shell per rendered palette entity; their fields/lanes/empties/inspectors contain none; the other sixteen compositions have Card count `0`. Every retained shell has the exact data tuple `{size:"sm", material:"content", tier:"quiet", surface:"opaque", shadow:false, grain:false, specular:"off"}` at every viewport. It is noninteractive container housing with no Card-level selection variant: it owns no activation, focus or selection state, and palette-Card `[inert]` count is `0` so its live child remains reachable. One native named `<button type="button" aria-pressed="false|true">` child spans specimen/identity and owns the selected register and focus; Card/article roots and palette seats expose no `aria-selected`, `role="option"`, listbox or composite-selection fiction. A seat is pressed iff its palette ID equals the selected inspector identity, no more than one is pressed, and the no-selection state has all seats false plus no inspector. An optional reorder handle is a separate named control. Region presence never implies a line. The following is the complete binding inventory: `[]` is the explicit empty P122 boundary set; `none` is the exact P122 reserve value; `n/a` means the composition is deliberately not a P122 consumer. Picker's rare legal-width wrap uses content flow below the readout inside the stage and does not turn on a persistent chassis reserve. A Card edge, Dialog edge, material change, focus ring or specimen-well edge is housing/state—not a divider—and is not counted as a retained line here.
