## Goal

Unify gradient stops, perceived result, code and timing into one usable authoring bench with a proportionate first-class easing stage.

## Scope and file bounds

First-class `/gradient` and `/easing` feature bodies; Gradient model/visualizer/stop/code/perceived-space components; Easing catalogue/picker/specimen/stage; keyframes/glass easing adoption; shared-axis, numeric and keyboard alternatives. W19 owns their route records and Dock affordances.

## Triumvirate and worktree

- **Specification:** `VISUAL-CONSTITUTION.md §4.2/§5.2/§7 Gradient/Easing`, `OPTICAL-BENCH-COMPOSITIONS.md §3`, B-18/B-19, BI P051/P122.

- **Implementation owner:** Gradient and Easing leads on disjoint subtrees with one model/integration owner.

## Current RED

The gradient was owner-declared unusable, controls/rail proportion remain weak, stop edits are pointer-heavy, and the easing selector is too small/broken relative to the keyframes curve instrument.

## Completion evidence

`/gradient` and `/easing` each load directly with their own route identity, P122 stage/inspector/action composition and no nested chassis or query-mode alias. Add/select/move/remove stops and select/edit easing via pointer, keyboard and numeric fields update the same gradient model/code where Gradient consumes Easing. Every stop's geometric seat—not its WatercolorDot face—owns selected/focused/grabbed state and the accessible name; no outline, ring, shadow register or pseudo-element crosses the organic edge in either scheme. Stop position and easing x/y controls follow their exact §5.2 LTR/RTL/Home/End rows; graphical handles and named numeric controls share one model. A stop crossing a neighbor is re-sorted atomically with its interval identity and announced ordinal, so array order, visual order and serialization never diverge. Every remaining axis adopts W21; only chromatic gradient domains are spectral, while time/easing is neutral with the specimen color moving through it. Preview dominates supporting controls; curve clamps 19–22rem and remains usable at 390; hatch/netting informs without obscuring; no old parser/facade or local EasingPicker path remains.
